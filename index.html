Shipping outside the US

Unfortunately, my print-on-demand provider, LuLu, doesn't provide a way to integrate with my electronic delivery provider, SendOwl. This means that I cannot have SendOwl fulfill the electronic version and LuLu fulfill the physical version, meaning I have to fulfill the physical orders myself.

Further, SendOwl provides no way to calculate shipping based on your address and, as I am in the US, shipping to countries outside the US is very expensive. To keep things simple for me, I'm initially just going to fulfill US orders, and roll in the cost of US shipping to the price.

If you still want the physical book

You can still purchase the physical book from LuLu directly using this link. LuLu will fulfill the order for you and ship to wherever you are. Since LuLu can't tell me who bought the book, I cannot send you a discount code on the electronic version to “simulate” the package deal (and I can't give you a discount code to LuLu either—they support it).

This won't necessarily be this way for ever, but I had to “cut scope” in order to ship. I'm sure this is a concept you are familiar with and I'm sorry I couldn't have sorted out something better.
